Maker's Space

What is a Maker Space?

A maker space is an environment in which resources are provides to encourge development of projects in engineering, computer science, entrepreneurship, or just for fun! By providing the tools needed to create prototypes and the space to discuss and develop ideas, the club promotes an environment for students to bring their ideas to life.

What Resources Are Available?

The club has a wide array of tools and resources available to its members including multiple 3D printers, an electronics repair kit, a wide array of hand tools and power tools, computers, and legos. Additionally, the club supervisor and club members are also invaluable resources to help develop ideas and troubleshoot!

3D Printing

3D printing is an invaluable way to create prototypes and concrete products. The club has access to multiple 3D printers and 3D modeling software. No expirience is needed and you can learn everything you need to know about how to 3D print!
